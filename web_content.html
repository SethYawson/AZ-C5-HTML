About State College

Agogo State Senior High School was established as a private institution in 1963 with twenty-eight students. The founding fathers aimed at following the principles of Prempeh College hence the popular name Agogo State College. As a result of the need for expansion, Agogo citizens voluntarily gave their farmlands and funds surcharged from cocoa proceeds towards the development of the new site.
